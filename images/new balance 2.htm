Details

New Balance's cult-classic 2002 model is back. This silhouette was first introduced in 2010 and now has been subtly updated and revived for the current generation. Now dubbed the 2002R, this modern iteration is a classic throwback. This colorway comes covered in breathable mesh and soft suede.

  • Suede upper
  • Mesh and leather underlays
  • "N" overlays at sides
  • Traditional lacing system
  • Padded tongue with New Balance branding
  • "2002R" printed at lateral heel
  • N-ergy foam midsole for support
  • Rubber outsole for traction

Color Shown: Black

Style: #M2002RJB

Shipping and Returns

Orders may take up to 48 business hours to ship. Once the order is shipped you will be emailed a tracking number. If you notice the tracking status reads "Label Created", that means the shipment has been paid for and it's awaiting the first "in-transit" scan by the carrier. Occasionally, shipping statuses can remain in the "Label Created" stage for 1-2 business days.
